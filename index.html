About The Gadfly

The Gadfly is an independent satirical newspaper. Student-first and student-run, whilst The Gadfly technically exists as a club at LHSS, we still operate mostly by ourselves (with oversight and occasional edits when required). We attempt to deliver amusing content, interesting ideas, and high-quality writing.

Our History

The Beginning - [May 2025]

The story of The Gadfly begins with 'The Backcast', a satirical newspaper with largely the same spirit as The Gadfly, but unrefined (and unsanctioned). Our first issue was released in May 2025.

The Fall - [June 2025]

The Backcast continued the next month. In June 2025, issue #2 of The Backcast was published. Due to a combination of effects (being unsanctioned, printing slightly controversial things, putting up posters whilst being unsanctioned, etc.), The Backcast was formally shut down.

The Rebirth - [Summer Break 2025]

After a small party to celebrate the life & times of The Backcast, we decided to continue the paper officially. We originally planned on calling our paper 'The Outcast', but out of concern that the name might sound too similar to Backcast or Forecast, we decided to pivot entirely. And, like that, The Gadfly was born. We spent the summer crafting our founding docs and writing our first issue.

The Gadfly Today - [September 2025]

With our heads held high, our newspaper and founding docs in hand, we met with our principal as soon as possible. He graciously allowed us to continue, even with our stipulations of not being a club in the traditional manner (not allowing people in freely, no club fair, etc.); we thank him greatly for this. We found a teacher sponsor who meshed well with The Gadfly and, after minor revisions and edits, we published our first issue under The Gadfly name.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does it look like 'The Badfly'?

You're probably just bad at reading.

What is your relation to The Forecast?

We aren't competitors, nor are we collaborators; we're complementary papers with different goals.

Why the name 'The Gadfly'?

The name Gadfly was chosen because we wanted to call ourselves something more pompous than 'Outcast'. We landed on the term that Socrates used when on trial for his life, having been accused of corrupting the youth.

How often do you publish?

We try to publish new issues monthly. Check out our archive to read past issues.

Can I contribute to The Gadfly?

No, probably not. You can try applying if you want, though: contact us.

Is everything you publish satire?

For now, yes. This might change in the future; we aren't sure quite yet.
